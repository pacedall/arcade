WHAT IS PACEDALL ARCADE?

Pacedall Arcade is a collection of small, timeless retro arcade games inspired by the golden age of the 1970s and 80s — rebuilt for modern devices by artificial intelligence.

Every game is designed, coded, and balanced entirely by AI as part of the Pacedall Labs product suite. Think Pac-Man, Space Invaders, Snake, Breakout — the classics reimagined with crisp pixel art and tight controls, no bloat, no complexity.

We believe great games don't need accounts, subscriptions, or your email address. Pick a game. Play. That's it.
